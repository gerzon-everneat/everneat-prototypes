About the role

Acme Corp is looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our growing design team. You'll work closely with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders to craft experiences that millions of users rely on every day. This role sits at the center of our product org and has direct impact on our core product roadmap.

We're a remote-friendly team that values autonomy, clear communication, and shipping quality work. You'll have the freedom to define processes, influence design systems, and raise the bar for craft across the organization. We move fast, but never at the expense of thoughtfulness or accessibility.

Our product serves over 2 million professionals across 40+ countries. Every design decision you make is an opportunity to improve the daily work lives of real people. We take that responsibility seriously, and we hope you do too.

If you care deeply about the intersection of utility and delight, have strong opinions loosely held, and thrive in collaborative, low-ego environments — we'd love to hear from you.

What you'll do

  • Lead end-to-end design for one of our core product areas — from discovery through delivery and iteration
  • Collaborate with PMs and engineers in weekly planning cycles to define scope, identify edge cases, and ship polished experiences
  • Contribute to and evolve our design system, ensuring components are accessible, scalable, and well-documented
  • Run user research sessions, synthesize findings, and present insights to product and leadership teams
  • Mentor junior designers through structured critique, design reviews, and pair-design sessions

Nice to have

  • Experience designing for B2B SaaS products or productivity tools with complex information architectures
  • Familiarity with front-end development (HTML/CSS basics) and ability to collaborate closely with engineers on implementation details
  • Previous experience building or maintaining a design system at scale
