Job Description

We're looking for a skilled HVAC Technician to join the Sunshine Property Group team. You'll be responsible for maintaining, repairing, and installing HVAC systems across our residential and commercial portfolio in Miami-Dade County.

Responsibilities

  • • Install, maintain, and repair HVAC systems in residential and commercial properties
  • • Diagnose equipment faults and perform corrective maintenance
  • • Handle refrigerant safely in compliance with EPA 608 regulations
  • • Complete service records and communicate updates to property managers
  • • Available for on-call emergency repairs (rotational)

Requirements

  • • 5+ years of HVAC experience
  • • EPA Section 608 certification (required)
  • • Valid Florida driver's license and reliable transportation
  • • Ability to lift 50+ lbs and work in confined spaces
